  • CIPE PINELES ARTIST BOOK
  • 5.5" x 8.5"
  • Perfect bound on Neenah paper
  • 2024

This artist book, highlighting graphic designer Cipe Pineles, was created to showcase the designer's life and the accomplishments throughout her career. As a pioneer for graphic design in the 1900s with elegant layout and editorial design, Cipe Pineles is a creative that I admire. With this artist book the typographic style is intended to represent the magazine editorial look while also incorporating her rustic gouache illustrations. This book includes font matter, a timeline, and back matter that is structured around a grid system. The timeline is extended across the book which breaks into page headers to represent the length and quality of the Pineles' career. The result – a "mini-magazine" style book which spans 58 total spreads.
