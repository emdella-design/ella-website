  • RECIPE BOOK
  • 6" x 9"
  • Laser-print on Hammermill paper
  • 2024

This book is a collection of my favorite recipes that I have created or have been passed down to me. My personal relationship with food has always been closely tied to community, and every dish here carries a story of the people who shaped it. By gathering these recipes to create this book, I hope to share my love of cooking in a way that feels welcoming, personal, and easy for anyone to enjoy.

Here are some of my artboards I created in Adobe Illustrator. As part of the process, I cooked and baked my recipes in order to photograph them as references. Through this process I felt very connected to my recipes.
