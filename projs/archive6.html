  • A SUMMER OF ART
  • 4" x 9" rack cards, 1080 x 1080 px IG posts
  • 2024

In celebration of the opening of the summer Sheafe Warehouse gallery, this rack card was made to highlight the seasonal exhibitions for the New Hampshire Art Association. Using Adobe Illustrator, the graphics were created using bright and warm summer colors to create a playful and inviting rack card. Over 500 cards were distributed around the Portsmouth city including the historical society, the market square, and the galleries.
