  • LOCAL HARVEST
  • 2025

Local Harvest is a website for community building where farmers, gardeners, artisans, and anyone looking to support local businesses can share their products on one platform. The mission is simple: to make it easier for people to find, share, and celebrate local food and handmade goods straight from the source. Farm stands and markets today can be hard to find, especially when local farms aren't familiar with social media. On Local Harvest, users are able to select a location and search for seasonal products, farms, and farmer's markets before they go to purchase.
