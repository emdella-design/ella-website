  • OCEAN CURRENTS INFOGRAPHIC
  • 11" x 17"
  • Matte inketjet print
  • 2024

This infographic is designed to educate a general audience about ocean currents, an extremely relevant part of the Earth and its climate. This topic is especially important because as polar ice continues to melt, these circulation systems are at increasing risk. This infographic was created by an in-depth research process including both written and visual sources, illustrationss, and refining multiple drafts. A combination of graphics and text make the infographic more engaging and accessible.
